# PilotQA AI - Product Vision

Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

PilotQA AI is an AI-powered Test Management Platform designed to simplify and accelerate the complete Software Testing Life Cycle (STLC).

The platform combines Test Management, Defect Management, Reporting, and Artificial Intelligence into a single enterprise-grade solution.

Our goal is to reduce manual effort, improve software quality, and help QA teams deliver faster with confidence.

---

# Mission

To build the world's most intelligent QA platform that assists testers instead of replacing them.

PilotQA AI will use Artificial Intelligence to automate repetitive testing activities while allowing testers to focus on quality engineering and business validation.
